Rank 10 Begleitpanzer 57

Historical Background

The Begleitpanzer 57 is a variant of the production Marder IFV, featuring enhanced armament in the form of a 57-mm Bofors automatic cannon, which allowed it to confidently destroy light armored vehicles at long distances. It was developed by Thyssen-Henschel and Bofors in 1977. To combat heavy armor, it was equipped with a TOW ATGM launcher, with the possibility of upgrading to the I-TOW. According to available data, only one prototype was built, and the project did not proceed beyond the testing phase.

Brief Overview

Maneuverability

Pros: Excellent mobility based on the Marder 1 chassis. The powerful diesel engine (600 hp) provides good acceleration, a top speed of about 83 km/h on paved roads, and a reverse speed of around 83 km/h. Decent power-to-weight ratio (~21 hp/t). A fully tracked platform ensures good cross-country performance.

Cons: Neutral steering (turning on the spot) is quite slow.

Armor and Survivability

Pros: The front of the hull and turret have anti-projectile resistance against small-caliber automatic cannons (20-30 mm). It can withstand fire from IFVs, SPAAGs, and aircraft. Layout features 4 crew members spaced throughout the hull.

Cons: Absolutely defenseless against any tank shells (APFSDS, HEAT, HE) and ATGMs. The sides and rear can be penetrated even by heavy machine guns (12.7+ mm).

Armament

Main gun: 57-mm Bofors L/70 Mk.1 automatic cannon. A unique and powerful weapon. High rate of fire: about 120 rounds/min in full automatic mode.

Shells:

57x438 AP-T: The main armor-piercing shell with explosive filler against various types of vehicles. Effective when shooting enemies in the side, especially heavy MBTs, as well as against medium MBTs.

57x438 HE-T: High-explosive fragmentation shell. Effective against lightly armored vehicles such as SPAAGs, IFVs, etc.

57x438 SAP-T: Semi-armor-piercing high-explosive shell. Also effective against lightly armored vehicles like SPAAGs, IFVs, etc.

57x438 HE-VT: High-explosive shell with a proximity fuze. Highly effective against aircraft.

I-TOW (ATGM): 6 guided anti-tank missiles. The best ATGM available for the Begleitpanzer 57, boasting 630mm of penetration. Effective against any type of vehicle.

7.62-mm machine gun.

Features

Equipped with a stabilizer. Very good gun depression/elevation angles (-8/+45). Excellent horizontal targeting speed (67.3°).

Conclusions and Comments

The Begleitpanzer 57 is not just an IFV, but a universal support vehicle. It is one of the most multifunctional and tactically flexible vehicles in the game. Its role is to control the airspace, fight light vehicles, and deliver precise strikes against heavy targets from a distance.
