Rank 7 2S19M1

Historical Background

The 2S19M1 "Msta-SM1" is a Soviet/Russian 152mm self-propelled howitzer built on a tracked chassis, developed as a further modernization of the "Msta-S" family. The M1 model is an improved version featuring modernized ballistics, an autoloader, advanced navigation and sighting systems, and updated ammunition logistics. In the game, it is represented as a versatile medium-to-long-range SPG with a high rate of fire and improved mobility compared to its predecessors.

Quick Overview

Maneuverability

Pros: Good mobility for an SPG—its mass and driving characteristics allow it to change positions promptly. It has a reasonably high top speed and handling on roads and light off-road terrain.

Cons: Limited capability in heavy off-road conditions and tight maneuvers in urban environments. Transitioning to a firing position and camouflaging takes time due to the deployment period of the gun.

Armor and Survivability

Pros: The armor layout features many angles, offering a decent chance for ricochets.

Cons: Light-to-medium armor—vulnerable to direct hits from tank guns and AT weaponry. It lacks comprehensive protection against aircraft and flank attacks once detected. There is no dedicated ammunition blowout compartment—direct hits are critical.

Armament

Primary: 152mm 2A64/2A65 rifled howitzer (modified for the M1). In-game parameters: fire rate with the autoloader is increased—cycle is approximately 6–8 rounds/min (depending on mode and modules); priority is placed on long-range fire and guided munitions. Elevation/depression angles meet standard SPG classes, but stabilization is limited—stationary positioning is required for accurate fire.

Ammunition:

Selection includes a solid 150mm APHE shell, an excellent HE shell, and a new addition for this rank: the HE-VT (Radio Fuse) for anti-aircraft use. I recommend a loadout of 12 APHE/HE shells and 5 HE-VT shells (if you plan to engage aircraft). HEAT shells are standard.

Key Features:

High efficiency in fire support and destroying fortified targets at medium-to-long ranges; the autoloader ensures a steady cycle of fire.

Conclusions and Comments

The 2S19M1 is a powerful modern SPG with a good rate of fire and range, capable of effectively supporting offensives and suppressing enemy attacks. It is vulnerable once detected and in close combat with tanks; play from a distance, occupy concealed firing positions, use scouts, and change your location quickly after firing. Essentially, it is a "glass cannon" that can hit very hard. I recommend researching it to get to the *Shturm-S* (which is also specific but good in its own way). This vehicle is best suited for enthusiasts who enjoy artillery mechanics due to its complex shell choice and vulnerability.
