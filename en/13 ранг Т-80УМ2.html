Rank 13 T-80UM2

Historical Background

The T-80UM2 is one of the variants of the further development of the T-80U tanks, produced by the Omsk Transport Engineering Plant. As part of the T-80UM modernization program, one of the prototypes was equipped with the "Drozd" active protection system. After the demonstration of the only built T-80UM-2 in 1997, the vehicle did not enter serial production.

Active Protection System (APS)

Since "Drozd" is an early system, it provides protection only against anti-tank guided missiles (ATGMs) and slow, high-caliber HEAT projectiles.

The launchers are stationary on the turret and only cover the turret's orientation. A positive feature is the relatively large ammunition count, specifically 8 charges.

However, keep in mind that each pair of launchers only covers its specific sector. This means that after a deployment, vulnerable gaps may form, and subsequent hits will cause damage.

In-Game Characteristics Information

The primary difference from the base version (T-80U) is the presence of the "Drozd" active protection system. Therefore, we recommend reading the existing article on the T-80U, as there are practically no other differences in gameplay.
