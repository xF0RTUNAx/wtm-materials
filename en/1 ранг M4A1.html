Rank 1 M4A1

Historical Background

The M4A1 is an early modification of the American Sherman family of medium tanks, featuring a cast hull with an improved armor shape and rounded surfaces. Production of the vehicle began in 1942. The tank became one of the most mass-produced and reliable among the Allies in World War II due to its high maintainability, crew comfort, and versatility. In War Thunder Mobile, the M4A1 retains its historical image — it is a balanced and versatile tank, suitable for both beginners and experienced players.

Brief Overview

Maneuverability

Its maneuverability allows for dynamic gameplay, but mistakes in positioning are punished quite severely.

Pros: Good forward speed. Confident dynamics on roads and hard surfaces. Excellent gun depression angles, allowing effective use of terrain. Comfortable turret traverse speed.

Cons: Noticeably loses speed on soft terrain. Weak reverse speed — makes it difficult to retreat quickly after firing.

Armor and Survivability

Pros: The cast hull with rounded shapes improves ballistics — ricochets are possible. The turret, at favorable angles, can sometimes withstand hits from small-caliber guns.

Cons: Frontal armor is weak — most guns of its rank penetrate it without much trouble. Sides and rear are extremely vulnerable. The large silhouette increases the chance of being hit.

When penetrated: Fatal hits to the ammunition rack often occur, leading to the instant destruction of the vehicle. The enemy frequently incapacitates a significant part of the crew, rendering the tank combat-ineffective or completely immobilizing it.

Armament

75 mm M3 gun

Pros: Comfortable accuracy and predictable ballistics. High rate of fire — about 5 seconds with fully upgraded crew skills. Excellent vertical guidance angles, convenient for shooting from elevated positions. Effective against light and medium tanks of its rank. The presence of a single-plane stabilizer improves firing comfort on the move.

Cons: Limited armor penetration — against heavy targets, aiming for weak spots is required. Damage is inconsistent; a precise hit is critically important. At long distances, it is more difficult to reliably penetrate armored targets.

Features

Very comfortable when playing from the terrain. Versatile: capable of operating both on the front line and in a support role. Requires careful use of cover. Excellent for beginners mastering the basics of positional play.

Conclusions and Comments

The M4A1 is a solid and comfortable early-rank tank in War Thunder Mobile, showing consistent results in the hands of both beginners and experienced players. Suitable for dynamic and maneuverable gameplay. Effective on hilly and rough terrain maps. Unforgiving in direct shootouts due to weak armor. Requires smart positioning and careful use of cover.
