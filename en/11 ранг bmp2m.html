Rank 11 BMP-2M

Historical Background

The BMP-2M "Berezhok" is a Russian modification of the BMP-2, developed in the early 2000s by the Instrument Design Bureau (KBP). The main difference between the BMP-2M and the standard BMP-2 lies in the new B05Ya01 "Berezhok" combat module, which, in addition to the standard 30-mm 2A42 automatic cannon, includes four "Kornet" ATGM launchers, an AG-30 30-mm automatic grenade launcher, and a modern fire control system with a second-generation gunner's thermal sight and a commander's panoramic sight. Serial production of the "Berezhok" combat module was set up at the Izhevsk Mechanical Plant. However, until 2017, only Algeria modernized its BMP-2s to the BMP-2M level, after which Russia followed suit.

Brief Overview

Maneuverability

Pros: Overall mobility for a vehicle of this type feels average, but it is sufficient for comfortable and active gameplay.

Cons: Slow reverse speed and large dimensions, which negatively affect handling.

Armor and Survivability

Armor and survivability metrics are standard for representatives of this vehicle class — meaning they practically do not exist. An unsuccessful enemy shot might save you. Sometimes, the large hull contributes to prolonged survival. Heavy machine guns and aircraft armament also leave little to no chance.

Armament

The automatic cannon is a decent solution, but you may have to spend quite a bit of time to fully destroy an enemy using only the gun. It is suitable for lightly armored targets, the sides of NATO tanks, and, of course, destroying critical enemy modules. There is an option to carry two belts, which is often enough. You will also frequently find the gun depression (aiming down) lacking.

Turret traverse and targeting speeds are close to those of an SPAA, which is definitely a plus and crucial in active combat.

It carries 4 Kornet ATGMs that can be fired on the move. There are also 4 more in reserve for reloading. The Kornets perform excellently in battle, aside from their flight speed, but due to familiar game quirks, strange outcomes can sometimes occur.

The auxiliary machine gun (grenade launcher) is only useful for destroying obstacles in order to save primary ammo belts.

Features

Dual fire control system. This vehicle is amphibious if needed.

Conclusions and Comments

Overall, the BMP is situational, but at the right moment, it can perform excellently. A more stealthy and patient gameplay style with elements of active maneuvers suits it best, but definitely not head-on engagements. It unlocks its full potential in the hands of more experienced players. One of the best and highest-ranking vehicles in its class, and quite versatile as well (support, anti-air duties, spawn protection, adequate and timely assaults, etc.).
